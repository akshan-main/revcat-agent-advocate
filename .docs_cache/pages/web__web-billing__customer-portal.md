Web Billing (formerly RevenueCat Billing) comes with a no-code customer portal for customers to manage their own subscriptions.

## Accessing the customer portal

Each email (subscription confirmation, renewal, billing issue) that Web Billing sends to customers contains a link for the customer to access their customer portal:

![](/docs_images/web/web-billing/email-link-customer-portal.png)

*Interactive content is available in the web version of this doc.*

## Managing subscriptions through the customer portal

In the customer portal, customers can do the following:

- See information about an upcoming payment
- Cancel a subscription
- Change their subscription to a different product, if configured (see [subscription changes](product-setup#configure-subscription-changes))
- Re-subscribe between cancellation and expiration
- Update the payment method for their subscription
- View a list of past payments
- Download PDF receipts and invoices for past payments
